Welcome to Junie's!

Junie's was created in 2013 by June Styles June grew up in the kitchen with her mom and grandmother. Her favorite time of year was the holidays, because her mom and grandmother baked the best sweet treats. With her mom and grandmother's influence, after graduating highschool, June traveled to Seattle to go to culinary school, where she focused her time on deluctable desserts. Among her time at culinary school, June started making cupcakes for her family and friends' special events. Word eventually got around and next thing you know June was baking cupcakes as her full time job, and Junie's was born. After a couple of years, June opened her first shop in 2015 in Seattle. Since then, Junie's has opened 2 other locations and continues to take the cupcake business by storm.
